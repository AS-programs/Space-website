Project Gemini

The gemini program was a human spaceflight program by nasa which lasted from 1961 to 1966.It carried 16 astronauts
to LEO.

Gemini capsule

The gemini capsule was an upgraded,bigger version of the mercury capsule.Howerver unlike the mercury capsule it had its oxygen,water and propulsion
in the adapter module(the white part) which detached before reentry.Also unlike mercury the gemini capsule's launch abort system did not use solid
rocket motors.Instead they had ejection seats.This was a pretty bad idea as the ejection seats firing could have killed the crew as the cabin was filled
with pure oxygen whereas when nasa tested the capsule abort it was filled with nitrogen.(this lesson was learnt the hard way in apollo 1 mission).

The adaptor module had 2 modules-equipment module ,retro module.
The equipment module contained the OAMS(Orbital Altitude and Manuevering System) which had sixteen thrusters for pitch,
yaw,roll and translation control.
The retro module contained four solid-fuel TE-M-385 Star-13E retrorockets which were used to deorbit the capsule.

Initially nasa had planned to land the capsule using a rogallo wing but it turned out to be too unreliable and hard to control so they switched it out
for the good old parachute touchdown at sea.


Agena target vehicle-

The agena vehicle was an uncrewed spacecraft made to test orbital revendouz and docking
for NASA's gemini program.NASA needed to practice docking in space for its upcoming apollo
missions.The agena spacecraft was used in 7 missions out of which 2 were successful.In the
Gemini 10 mission the agena vehicle used its thrusters to boost the gemini spacecraft
to an orbit with an apogee of 763km and later another agena vehicle boosted gemini 11
to an apogee of 1369km.


Missions-

  • Gemini 4 had the first american EVA which was performed by Ed White on June 3rd,1965.
  • Gemini 6A along with gemini 7 performed the first space revendouz.This required some
    very precise orbital manuevers.
  • Gemini 8 under the command of Neil Armstrong achieved the first space docking.It docked
    with an Agena vehicle.However soon after docking it started rotating fastly.Armstrong thought it
    was a thruster malfunction on the Agena vehicle and undocked the gemini.However then it began
    to rotate faster as it no longer help the mass of the agena vehicle.This forced armstrong the engage
    the reentry thrusters.This mission was the first american emergency landing. Luckily none of the
    astronauts were hurt.
  • Gemini 11 was the highest earth orbit mission.It went upto 1,369.0 km which was the highest
    of any non-apollo manned spacecraft
