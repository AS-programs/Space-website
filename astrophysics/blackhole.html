What are Black holes?

A black hole is a celestial body .A black hole consists of a point of infinite density
which bends space time so much that it causes time dilation and even light is not able
to escape the gravitational pull of a black hole once it gets inside the event horizon.

How do black holes form?

Most black holes form from giant and supergiant stars(these are the types of stars with 10-30 solar masses).
When a star burns it converts hydrogen into helium in a process known as nuclear fusion.Most stars turn into dwarf
stars once their hydrogen runs out.However some massive stars continue fusion and fuse helium into carbon,carbon
into neon,neon into oxygen,oxygen into silicon,silicon into iron.Once iron forms the core of the star is no longer
able to continue fusion.Due to the lack of fusion the core can no longer produce an outwards force to resist its own
gravity so the core collapses.The outer layers of the star explode violently in a supernova.Meanwhile in the core the
protons and electrons merge together the form neutrons.The only thing stopping this from colliding further is the
neutron degeneracy force which prevents any 2 neutrons from coming together at the same point.However in some cases
the star is so massive that even the neutron degeneracy force cannot stop the core from collapsing and all the matter
in the core collapses to an infinitesimally small point called a singularity.U have now got a black hole.

Gravitational lensing

The theory of general relativity tells us that gravity is caused by the
bending of a 4 dimensional fabric of spacetime.when light passes through
the bend spacetime its path gets curved and it may appear distorted.In black
holes this fabric of spacetime is bent a lot due to their high density.This
causes light to appear distorted around the black hole.









ISCO and ICO

Genral relativity describes a concept called Innermost Circular Stable Orbit(ICSO) which tells the minimum orbital
radius of an orbiting body(with a circular orbit) for a stable orbit.If this orbiting body were to come any closer
it would become unstable and spiral into the black hole.Only black holes and neutron stars have the ICSO out of
their surface.AS these spin faster the ICSO decreases.
ICO is the innermost cicular orbit and it is the minimum possible orbital radius of an orbiting body with a circular
orbit.
