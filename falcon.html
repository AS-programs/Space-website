Falcon 1

Falcon 1 was an expendable launch vehicle privately developed and manufactured by spacex.It had its first flight
on 24th march 2006 which failed due to an engine failure.These were followed by another failures caused by harmonic
oscillations,premature engine shutdown and its third failure was caused by residual stage 1 thrust leading to collision
of first and second stages immediately after stage separation.Its first successful flight was on 3 August 2008 and carried
a mass simulator.Its final flight was the only commercial flight of the falcon 1 which carrid the malaysian RazakSAT to LEO.


Falcon 9

The falcon 9 is a partially reusable launch vehicle manufactured and operated by spacex.It is the most flown rocket in this
decade with upto 50 launches per year.After stage separation the first stage soon performs a reentry burn and then uses its
grid fins to manuever around.Then it performs a "suicide burn".Falcon 9 is not cabale of hovering(having a TWR of 1) so it has
to perform a "last minute" burn to quickly cancel out its downwards velocity.Then it deploys its landing legs and performs a
landing on a ground pad or a droneship(A Shortfall Of Gravitas(ASOG) or Of Course I Still Love You (OCISLY) or Just Read the
Instructions (JRTI).The second stage continues on its path to orbit the after payload deploymeny it deorbits itself.

The falcon 9 has launched many mission including several starlink missions,1 interplanetary mission(DART probe),several
commercial mission,several iss cargo resupply mission(CRS)(using the dragon capsule) and 4 crewed missions(Crew 1,Crew 2
,Crew 3,Inspiration 4).Till now it has had 2 failed missions(CRS 7,AMOS 6).
