Little Joe 2

The little joe rocket was used to test the abort system of apollo capsule(similar to little joe 1 testing gemini capsule abort)

Saturn 1

The saturn 1 was the first american medium lift launch vehicle.The first stage had 8 redstone tanks aroound a jupiter tank.
Saturn 1 was used to test the boilerplate version of the apollo command and service module.

Saturn 1B

Saturn 1B was a 55 metre tall rocket that has 5 test flight,4 apollo test flights and one pegasus satellites launch.It was
basically an upgaded Saturn 1.The first and second stage were upgraded.The second stage S-4B also served as the third stage of
the saturn 5 rocket.

Saturn 5

The saturn 5 is the most powerful rocket ever launched by humans.It is probably the most famous rocket.It was used to launch
all the crewed moon missions.
