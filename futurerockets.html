Neutron rocket-

The neutron rocket is a partially reusable medium lift launch vehicle currently
being developed by rocket lab.It will be made out of carbon fibre.It consists of a
reusable first stage with 4 integrated fairings,and an expendable upper stage with
ayload on top of it stored in the fairings.In space the fairing will open up and
release the second stage and the payload and then the fairing will close again and
the first stage will perform a retrograde burn while reentry.Then it will use its
fins to manuever it towards the launch site.Then it will a landing burn to land at
the launch site.This is possible as the first stage will have very little horizontal
velocity at stage separation compared to falcon 9 first stage which has a lot of
horizontal velocity at stage separation and RTLS(Return To Launch Site) is usually
not feasible, so it lands on droneships usually.



Starship

Starship is a fully reusable super heavy lift launch vehicle currently in development.It is built by spacex and is expected
to have its first orbital flight this year(2022).The location for consturction of these prototypes is boca chica,texas,usa.
The rocket consists of the second stage(confusingly also called as starship) and the first stage(called as super heavy).
It is powered by the raptor engine which runs on full flow staged combustion cycle.Here 2 gas generators are used and both
fuel rich and oxygen rich fluid is mixed and ignited in combustion chamber.The super heavy will have 33 sea level raptor
engines and the starship will have 3 sea level and 3 or 6 vacuum raptor engines.
Upon ignition the rocket will liftoff.Once in space the starship will separate.Then it will use its 4 grid fins to manuever
back to launch site.Here it will light its engines and land on 2 big mechanical arms on a tower.This is done to save landing
legs mass.Meanwhile the starship will light its engines to reach orbit.Once its mission is done it will perform a small burn
to lower its perigee into the atmosphere.Then it will do a reentry.The front side of the starship is covered with heat shield
tiles to prevent it from heating too much and breaking apart.It will use its flaps to slow itself down using air resistance.
It will belly flop till its near the launch pad again.Then it will tilt its engines and perform a burn to tilt the starship
into an upright position and land it into the arms of the tower.The stages will then be checked,stacked again,and be ready to
be launched again.There is also a HLS(Human Landing System) variant of starship which nasa has payed spacex to develop for
landing astronaut back to the moon under the artemis mission.
