Project Mercury

The mercury program was america's first human spaceflight program.This program had 3 launch vehicles and involved sending few
chimpanzees and 7 astronauts to space and consisted of 26 total launches.

Launch Vehicle-

The mercury program had 3 launch vehicles-

The Mercury Atlas,Mercury Redstone,Little Joe rocket respectively.

The little joe rocket was a 17 metres launch vehicle used for testing the mercury capsule.Its main purpose
was to test the launch abort system at max q(max q is when a rocket experiences maximum aerodynamic forces).


The mercury redstone rocket was a 25.4 metres suborbital rocket.It was used to launch a chimpanzee named Ham
to space.It also launched the first american in space(Alan Shepard).


The mercury atlas was a 28.7 metres orbital rocket.It was used to launch a chimpanzee named Enos.It was used to
launch John Glenn(the first american in orbit) and 3 other crewed orbital flights.
