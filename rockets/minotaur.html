Minotaur launch vehicles

The minotaur rocket family consists of 6 american solid-fueled rockets.These rockets have been derived from the
minuteman and peacemaker ICBM's.This is the reason minotaur rockets have high TWR(Thrust to Weight Ratio) which
causes it to go up very fast as ICBM's need to deliver their payload quickly.
